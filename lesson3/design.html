Typography Choices

For this website, I chose to use the Times New Roman font. This is a site that aims to draw in and provide service to professionals in every realm and industry. That professionalism should be reflected in the font choice. Times New Roman is a simplistic, common, and efficient font choice because it will get the job done without distracting target audience members from the purpose of their visit.

Color Schema

I chose to use a calming and simple green color schema for this site. The color schema should not distract visitors with multiple bright colors, as this could convey a lack of professionalism or focus on our end. The green color also flows well with the banner image and creates a feeling a visual completeness while maintaining simplicity in design. The white colored text that has been applied to the head, nav, and footer text really helps the text pop out from the green background. White is also very neutral and doesn't clash with the color choices in other elements of the site.
