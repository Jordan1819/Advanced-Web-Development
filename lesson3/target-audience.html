Our Target Audience

Our target audience members are individuals and businesses that are invested in long-term growth, networking, and cooperation in the Idaho Falls area. This region is growing rapidly and the increased development and growth will create boundless opportunities and prosperity. If you are looking for team members that are as invested in your success as you are, then you've come to the right place.

Persona Profiles

    Persona 1: Chief Marketing Director

  • Demographic: 48 year old female with a masters degree in business management.
  • Goals: Increase company outreach, client acquisition, and profit margins.

    Persona 2: Founder of Startup Company

  • Demographic: 27 year old male entrepreneur with no business connections or experience.
  • Goals: End the fiscal year with notable profits, improve networking and business strategies, and gain professional allies and support.

Possible Scenarios

  • How can I network with other local companies?
  • Can I contact the Chamber of Commerce to ask questions?
  • What local events are coming up that I can advertise at?
  • How can I connect with other professionals in my industry and demographic?
  • Are there testimonials of the Chamber of Commerce that I can read?
