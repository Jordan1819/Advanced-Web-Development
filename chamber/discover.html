    Discover the beauty and serenity of Idaho Falls

    Dining

    Idaho Falls is home to many delectable dining destinations. As the cultural, commercial, and healhcare center of Eastern Idaho, our local restaurants and diners offer a vast assortment of high-quality dishes from all over the world. Idaho Falls is known for our fantastic local Mexican restaurants, with Pachanga's topping the list with hundreds of glowing Google reviews. Whether you're in the mood for authentic sushi, thai-style curry, good old barbeque ribs, or delicious local Mexican food, you will find what you're looking for!

    Local Farmer's Market

    Our town hosts a unique and energetic farmers market during the beautiful summer months. The market is open from 9am-1pm every Saturday from May 1st through October 30th. Many local restaurants and businesses fill the street offering unique products and delicious food and drink right next to the beautiful Snake River. This beloved local tradition is a great way for individuals and local businesses to familiarize themselves with the community and create lasting connections and memories.

    Beautiful Hiking and Walking Trails

    If exploring the outdoors is your forte, Idaho Falls is the perfect base camp for you. The city itself is home to 8 unique, beautiful, and scenic hiking routes and there are over 20 hiking trails within an hour drive of the city. If you find yourself short on gear, there are many local businesses like Idaho Mountain Trading, Cabela's, and Sportsman's Warehouse that can meet your every need. Come explore the beautiful country that Idaho has to offer!

    "Alive After 5" Music and Nightlife

    Idaho Falls is very proud of "The Civitan Club of Idaho Falls" who organize our Alive after 5 events every summer. Alive after 5 is a weekly fundraiser program where the community gets together to enjoy food, drink, and music while raising money for good causes. The funds gathered benefit local playgrounds, individuals with disabilities, education scholarships, and more. This program is a great opportunity for businesses to increase their outreach to the community and create lasting relationships and connections.

    Idaho Falls Chukars Baseball

    We are the proud home of the Idaho Falls Chukars baseball team which was founded in 1940. The Chukars are an independant team in the Pioneer MLB Partner League. The Chukars are a farm team for the Kansas City Royals and have some incredible past and present athletes, many of which have become or are on track to joining the Major Leagues. The only professional sports team in Eastern Idaho competes from May through September. Come enjoy a ball game at the beautiful Melaleuca Field!
